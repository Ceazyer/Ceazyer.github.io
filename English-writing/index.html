英语作文模板总结

信件类

开头

How are you? I haven’t seen you for a while. Hope all as well.

Anyway, I’m writing because I want to …

结尾

I’m look forward to hearing from you soon. Take care and see you soon.

感谢信

thank you vary much for …, It’s hard for me to forget that you did at the time.

This is to tell you again how much I appreciate you. Every moment was a delight …

You must give me the chance to thank you.

invite you over for …, and I think you’d like to join.

交代时间+地点+活动内容

交代会如何安排流程

ask for your kindness assistance.

交代请求内容

交代请求原因与需求

Thank you so much for your generous help in this matter.

congratulations upon having …

I konw this is the result of your efforts.

I have followed this going with pleasure and interest.

I’m sure, from your efforts, you will be a success in whatever you undertake.

告示类

Notice/Announcement

开头

  • It is notice/ announced/ made public that …
  • Owing to/ due to/ because of/ in order to …, we beg to inform you that …
  • Wellcom to …

中间

You can be involved in a variety of activities, including sth.
These activities are high beneficial in multiple regards. First, … . Second, … .

结尾

联系方式/注意事项

If you are interested in this activity, please fell free to contact us via 联系方式. The deadline is 日期. Come and join us now!

大作文

开头

As is vividly revealed in the photo/drawing/cartoon,描述图像内容. We are informed that“图中的文字内容”.

  • The author’s real purpose is not the picture itself, but to lead us find what hides behind the iceberg.

作者有反讽意图用

  • In fact, what the author wants to express is very obvious.

作者想要表达的很明显

中间：提出观点后要提供相应的论据

Undoubtedly, we can deduce from the photo/drawing/cartoon that the author/cartoonist intends to attract our attention to the issue of 文章主题词.

主题中心句，围绕此句开始论述。

The point is best illustracted by the example of 中心点. we should know 举示例. Although … .

围绕中心词举例：我们应该清楚…。尽管…，但是…。

Why does this happen? I can think of no better reasons for the phenomenon other than the following two.

举例论证导致该现象发生的两个原因

结尾

From my perspective, …

阐述自己的观点

  • In terms of …, we should take advantages of the fruits and avoid the opposite facet.

正面/中立 话题

  • On the macro level, the goverment and the mass media need to shoulder their social responsibility to …

负面问题

To sum up, it is a positive/nagative trend and should be simulated/controlled, since it is beneficial not merely to individuals but to the whole society.

总之，积极应当被鼓励/消极应当被控制，对个人和社会都是有益的。
